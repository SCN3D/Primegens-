Database Support
PRIMEGENS also provide whole genome sequences for various model organism to check cross-hybridization of designed primers with any sequence in full genome of any organism. Supported genome databases are

  • Arabidopsis TAIR9 cDNA
  • Arabidopsis TAIR9 CDS
  • Maize CDS
  • Medicago CDS
  • Rice all cDNA
  • Rice all CDS
  • Sorghum all CDS
  • Soybean Glyma1 cDNA
  • Soybean Glyma1 CDS
  • Human Genome
  • Bisulfite Human Genome
  • Anopheles gambiae
  • Caenorhabditis_elegans
  • Drosophila Melanogaster
  • Guinea Pig
  • Mus musculus
  • Saccharomyces cerevisiae
  • Zebrafish
